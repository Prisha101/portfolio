Illustrations and Card Design




About the Project: Enola Holmes inspired Card Design


This project features a set of four illustrated card designs inspired by the movie “Enola Holmes”. Each card showcases a unique character from the film, brought to life through expressive illustrations. The artwork reflects the personalities and distinctive traits of the characters. This project highlights my approach to character-focused illustration and card design, creating a visually cohesive and engaging representation of the “Enola Holmes” movie.
