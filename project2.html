About the Project: Nutella Packaging Redesign with Echo Edge


This project reimagines the iconic Nutella packaging by incorporating the custom-designed “Echo Edge” font. Staying true to the brand’s original color palette of rich red, white, and brown, the redesign preserves the familiar warmth and approachability of Nutella while introducing a fresh, modern twist.

The “Echo Edge” font adds a dynamic and artistic flair to the packaging, creating a bold yet harmonious look that stands out on shelves. The unique shapes and fluid curves of the font align with Nutella's playful and indulgent personality, giving the packaging a contemporary update without losing its timeless appeal.

This redesign demonstrates how typography can transform a product’s visual identity while maintaining its essence, offering a blend of innovation and tradition.
